Campus Emergency Response Project

Alex Aquino, Roberto Duran, Nichole Mendez, Mackenzie Portie, & Destiny Sanchez

Background

Our group located some of the Blue Light Emergency phones on the Texas A&M University (TAMU) main campus. We created this webpage to include information on these phone systems such as the study area, crime awareness, and an interactive web map. This project aims to improve campus safety, provide accessibility, and promote emergency preparedness.

  • How were the locations picked?
      • The emergency phones were strategically distributed throughout campus, focusing on locations easily accessible to students, faculty, staff, and visitors. These locations include not just outside of most buildings on campus, but in parking garages as well.
  • Why are these emergency phones placed on campus?
      • The emergency phones allow users to have immediate contact with the 911 system. The phones can also be used for non-emergency situations, such as asking for a campus escort.
  • How do they work?
      • There are instructions located on the phone that should be followed in the case of an emergency. Once the phone is activated the University Police is able to access your location, knowing exactly where you are. Through the speaker on the phone, you can communicate directly with the dispatcher.

    Interactive Web Map

    Information about the map below:

    • Purpose- Our web map serves to provide easy accessibility and improve campus safety.
    • Data Source- The data displayed was publicly collected by our group.
    • Features- The emergency phones located around campus are mapped with distinguishable symbols along with the university police and nearby urgent care centers/hospitals.
    • Interactivity- Users can click on any of the emergency phones and a popup will appear that provides information of the nearest building along with a photo of that specific phone.
    • Usage- This web map is intended for students, faculty, and staff of TAMU as well as residents of College Station, TX.
